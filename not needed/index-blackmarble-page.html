Earth At Night

This is a Mapbox Visualization using the NASA Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite (VIIRS) instrument, a component of the Suomi National Polar-orbiting Partnership (NPP) satellite. VIIRS consists of 22 spectral bands from the ultra-violet to the mid-infrared, one of which is able to observe nighttime lights, the day night band (DNB). DNB is a panchromatic band sensitive to visible and near-infrared wavelengths.
